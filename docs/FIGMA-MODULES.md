# Figma Campaign Template Module Reference

Every graphic email in the campaign calendar should reference specific Figma modules by name in the design note. This gives the designer or builder an exact blueprint for what to pull from the template library.

Modules are organized by category. Each email typically uses 2-4 modules stacked together (e.g., a Hero + a Product Grid + a Trust Badge Strip + a Footer).

---

## Hero / Header Modules (8 modules)

These go at the top of the email. Every graphic email needs one.

### Holiday Sale Banner
Full-width seasonal headline treatment for major retail holidays (4th of July, BFCM, Cyber Monday, Christmas, Canada Day, Earth Day). Includes a scrolling ticker variant for high-energy sales events (e.g., "Black Friday . Black Friday . Black Friday...").

- **When to use:** Any holiday-themed sale email
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### Countdown Timer Hero
Large `00 : 00 : 00` countdown display showing Hours/Minutes/Seconds until offer expiry.

- **When to use:** Flash sales, BFCM, any email with a time-limited offer, launch day
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### Discount Code Hero
Large headline with a prominent code block and expiry line. Supports static codes or dynamic Klaviyo-generated codes.

- **When to use:** Any email featuring a discount code as the primary offer
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### BOGO Hero
Full-width header announcing a buy-one-get-one deal. "BUY ONE GET ONE [X]% OFF SITEWIDE" format.

- **When to use:** BOGO promotions
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### Urgency Banner
Headline strip with urgency messaging: "ENDS TONIGHT", "LAST CHANCE", "24 HOURS ONLY", "SALE EXTENDED".

- **When to use:** Final-push emails, sale extensions, last day of a promotion
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### New Drop Hero
Announcement header for new products, restocks, or new arrivals. Features product visual with "NEW DROP" / "RESTOCKED" / "NEW ARRIVALS" headline.

- **When to use:** Product launches, restocks, collection drops
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

### Standard Editorial Header
Clean logo + headline + subheadline layout. No heavy promotional treatment.

- **When to use:** Educational emails, brand story emails, content-forward emails
- **Best pillar pairing:** Educational, Community / Branded

### Gift Guide Hero
Seasonal gift guide header: "The Ultimate Gift Guide For [Him/Her/Them]" with festive or seasonal design treatment.

- **When to use:** Pre-holiday gifting emails (Mother's Day, Father's Day, Christmas, Valentine's Day)
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

## Offer Modules (9 modules)

These communicate the deal. Place them below the hero or as the primary content block in sale emails.

### Tiered Discount Block
Three-tier pricing structure. Example: 15% off orders $50+ / 20% off orders $100+ / 25% off orders $150+. Tiers are customizable.

- **When to use:** AOV-boosting promotions, "the more you buy the more you save" campaigns
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### BOGO Module
Product image with "BUY ONE GET ONE [X]% OFF" messaging and a CTA button.

- **When to use:** BOGO promotions at the product level
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### Doorbuster Deals Grid
Grid of 3-4 specific products, each with their own percentage discount and individual SHOP NOW buttons.

- **When to use:** BFCM doorbusters, curated sale selections, clearance events
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### Email Exclusive Split
Two-column layout splitting exclusive vs. general offers. "Email Exclusive [X]% / Everything Else [Y]%".

- **When to use:** Rewarding email subscribers with a better deal than the general site offer
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### Flash Sale Strip
Stacked tier display: "1 Day Only. Don't miss it! Orders $X+ get [X]% off."

- **When to use:** 24-hour or single-day flash sales
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### Offer Expiry Block
Urgency-driven offer block: "Your [X]% Off Expires Tonight" or "Your $[X] Offer Expires Soon" with code and CTA.

- **When to use:** Final reminder emails for expiring offers, abandoned browse follow-ups in campaigns
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### Klaviyo Dynamic Section
Personalized product recommendations or dynamic discount codes pulled from Klaviyo. Content changes per recipient.

- **When to use:** Personalized campaign sends, dynamic product recommendations
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection, Sales

### Price Strike-Through Block
Original price visually crossed out with the sale price next to it. Includes percentage-off badge. Example: "$280" crossed out, "$169", "-40%".

- **When to use:** Any email where showing the price drop is the key visual hook
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales

### Limited Stock Badge
"LIMITED STOCK" callout overlay placed on a product image.

- **When to use:** Low-inventory alerts, scarcity-driven emails, restock announcements with limited quantities
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection, Sales

## Educational / Content Modules (9 modules)

These teach, inform, or drive content engagement. The backbone of Educational pillar emails.

### Myth-Busting Numbered List
Numbered myths with check/X indicators per item. "Common [Category] Myths: 1, 2, 3..." format.

- **When to use:** Myth-busting angles, misconception-clearing emails
- **Best pillar pairing:** Educational

### Us vs. Them Comparison
Two-column comparison layout. "YOUR BRAND" with checkmarks vs. "The Other Guys" with X marks, with feature rows.

- **When to use:** Competitive differentiation, "why us" emails, ingredient/quality comparisons
- **Best pillar pairing:** Educational

### Numbered Tips Module
"Top [X] Tips: Diet/Recovery/Routine Edition" with numbered items, each with an icon or image.

- **When to use:** Tips lists, how-to content, routine guides
- **Best pillar pairing:** Educational

### Step-by-Step Routine
Sequential flow layout: "Step 1: [Action]", "Step 2: [Action]", etc. Good for multi-step processes.

- **When to use:** Skincare routines, haircare routines, supplement schedules, recipe steps, how-to guides
- **Best pillar pairing:** Educational

### Recipe Block
Recipe name with ingredient callouts and a "Get Recipe" CTA button.

- **When to use:** Food, beverage, or supplement brands with recipe content; seasonal recipe tie-ins
- **Best pillar pairing:** Educational

### Blog CTA Block
"READ THE BLOG" headline with a teaser sentence and CTA button. Drives traffic to owned content.

- **When to use:** When you have a blog post to promote, content marketing emails
- **Best pillar pairing:** Educational

### How It Works (4-Step)
Four-step visual flow: Select, Choose, Customize, Order (or any custom 4-step sequence).

- **When to use:** Explaining how a product, subscription, or process works
- **Best pillar pairing:** Educational, Product / Collection

### Tip Callout Block
Inline callout: "Tip: [short actionable tip]" with a check icon. Compact format for embedding tips within longer emails.

- **When to use:** Adding a quick educational element to a product or sales email
- **Best pillar pairing:** Educational

### Ingredient Callout Icons
Icon + benefit one-liner format. Example: an icon with "Promotes healthy estrogen levels" or "Reduces hormonal acne".

- **When to use:** Supplement, skincare, or clean-label brands highlighting specific ingredients and their benefits
- **Best pillar pairing:** Educational

## Social Proof Modules (8 modules)

These build trust through customer voices. Essential for Social Proof pillar emails but useful as supporting blocks in any email.

### Single Testimonial Full-Width
Large quote with reviewer name, Verified Buyer badge, and date. Takes up the full width for maximum impact.

- **When to use:** When you have one powerful testimonial to feature prominently
- **Best pillar pairing:** Social Proof

### Multi-Review Grid
Two or three testimonials displayed side by side with names and star ratings.

- **When to use:** Review roundup emails, social proof compilations
- **Best pillar pairing:** Social Proof

### Star Rating Bar
Trust strip showing aggregate review data: "[X,000]+ Reviews . [X.X] Average Rating".

- **When to use:** As a supporting element in any email to reinforce trust; especially effective below product grids
- **Best pillar pairing:** Social Proof, Product / Collection

### Customer Story Spotlight
Featured customer narrative format. One person's story told in detail with name and context.

- **When to use:** Dedicated customer story emails, transformation narratives
- **Best pillar pairing:** Social Proof

### Before / After Module
Side-by-side or stacked BEFORE and AFTER images showing product results.

- **When to use:** Skincare, haircare, fitness, or any transformation-driven product
- **Best pillar pairing:** Social Proof

### UGC Block
User-submitted photo with caption and verified buyer attribution.

- **When to use:** UGC roundup emails, community showcase
- **Best pillar pairing:** Social Proof, Community / Branded

### SPICY TESTIMONIALS
Fun, punchy review compilation styled with brand personality. Less formal than a standard review grid.

- **When to use:** Brands with a casual or bold tone; testimonial emails that need energy
- **Best pillar pairing:** Social Proof

### Repeat Buyer Badge
Trust callout near the CTA: "503+ Verified Reviews" or "267+ Verified Reviews".

- **When to use:** As a supporting element near any SHOP NOW button to reduce purchase hesitation
- **Best pillar pairing:** Social Proof, Product / Collection

## Product / Collection Modules (11 modules)

These showcase products. The core building blocks for Product/Collection pillar emails and supporting elements in Sales emails.

### Single Product Spotlight
Hero image with product name, short description, and SHOP NOW button. One product, full focus.

- **When to use:** New product launches, hero product features, restocks
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

### 2-Product Grid
Two products displayed side by side with names and individual CTAs.

- **When to use:** Product comparisons, "his and hers" pairings, complementary products
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

### 3-Product Grid
Three products (PRODUCT 1/2/3) with images and individual SHOP NOW buttons.

- **When to use:** Collection highlights, curated picks, "top 3" features
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

### 4-Product Grid
Four products in a grid layout. Maximum product density without overwhelming.

- **When to use:** Gift guides, collection launches, best sellers roundups, seasonal picks
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

### Best Sellers Callout
Header text ("The Top Picks" / "Check Out Our Best Sellers") followed by a product grid.

- **When to use:** Best sellers emails, monthly top picks, "most popular" features
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

### Shop By Category
Category navigation tiles: SHOP MEN'S / SHOP WOMEN'S / Kits & Supplies, etc.

- **When to use:** Emails driving general site exploration, new subscriber welcome campaigns, broad audience sends
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

### New Arrivals Strip
Navigation bar with NEW ARRIVALS / FINAL SALE / CONTACT US links.

- **When to use:** As a secondary navigation element in any email, especially near the footer
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

### Size Selector Display
Visual grid of available sizes: XXS / XS / S / M / L / XL / 2XL / 3XL / 4XL / 5XL.

- **When to use:** Apparel brands announcing inclusive sizing, new size availability, or restock by size
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

### Product Feature Breakdown
Feature #1 / Feature #2 / Feature #3 layout with icons and one-line descriptions per feature.

- **When to use:** Highlighting what makes a product unique, feature-by-feature walkthroughs
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection, Educational

### Add to Box Module
Product card with an "Add to Box" CTA button.

- **When to use:** Subscription brands encouraging add-ons to the next shipment
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection

### Bundle / Combo Module
Two products displayed with a "+" visual between them, positioned as a duo or bundle.

- **When to use:** Cross-sell emails, "better together" bundles, combo deals
- **Best pillar pairing:** Product / Collection, Sales

## Brand / Community Modules (6 modules)

These build brand affinity and community connection. Core modules for Community/Branded pillar emails.

### Founder Story Block
Founder name, image, and personal narrative. "How It All Began..." or "Our Story" format.

- **When to use:** Brand story emails, founder message emails, anniversary milestones
- **Best pillar pairing:** Community / Branded

### Brand Mission Block
Values-driven copy block. Formats like "GROW & BEHOLD", "Beauty With A Purpose", "MY MISSION".

- **When to use:** Mission-focused emails, cause marketing, brand values features
- **Best pillar pairing:** Community / Branded

### Giveaway Entry Block
Prize visual with contest description and an "ENTER HERE" CTA button.

- **When to use:** Giveaway and contest emails, engagement-driving campaigns
- **Best pillar pairing:** Community / Branded

### Referral Program Block
"Earn $20 when you refer a friend" messaging with an INVITE A FRIEND button.

- **When to use:** Referral program launches, re-engagement of existing referral programs, loyalty emails
- **Best pillar pairing:** Community / Branded

### Digital Gift Card Block
Gift card product image with gifting copy and CTA.

- **When to use:** Pre-holiday emails, last-minute gift reminders, "gift the hard-to-shop-for" angles
- **Best pillar pairing:** Community / Branded, Product / Collection

### Cause / Holiday Tie-In Block
Mission-aligned offer tied to a specific holiday or cause. Example: "In Honor of Earth Day -- Use code VIPEARTHDAY".

- **When to use:** Earth Day, Veterans Day, Canada Day, or any cause-aligned holiday where the brand donates or offers a themed discount
- **Best pillar pairing:** Community / Branded

---

## Logistics / Trust Modules (5 modules)

These reduce friction and build confidence. Use as supporting blocks in any email, especially sales and product emails.

### Shipping Guide / Deadline Block
Shipping cutoff dates with urgency: "GET-IT-IN-TIME" with standard shipping deadlines (e.g., "Order by Dec 12th for Dec 24th arrival").

- **When to use:** December emails (mandatory), any pre-holiday period where delivery timing matters
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales, Product / Collection

### Trust Badge Strip
Horizontal strip with trust signals: BUY NOW PAY LATER / FREE X DAY SHIPPING / 24/7 SUPPORT.

- **When to use:** As a supporting element in any email, especially effective below the hero or above the CTA
- **Best pillar pairing:** All pillars

### Customer Support Block
"Have a question? Contact us at support@[brand].com" block.

- **When to use:** Post-purchase emails, high-consideration product emails, any email where questions might prevent conversion
- **Best pillar pairing:** All pillars

### Buy Now Pay Later Block
Financing callout: "BUY NOW, PAY LATER WITH PAYPAL" or similar BNPL messaging.

- **When to use:** High-AOV product emails, cart-value-boosting campaigns
- **Best pillar pairing:** Sales, Product / Collection

### Free Shipping Banner
"FREE SHIPPING ON ALL ORDERS" or "Free shipping on orders $100+".

- **When to use:** As a persistent element in any email, or as a dedicated announcement when free shipping thresholds change
- **Best pillar pairing:** All pillars

---

## Footer Modules (3 modules)

Every email needs a footer. Pick one.

### Standard Footer
Unsubscribe link, copyright notice, and brand address. The default.

- **When to use:** Any email
- **Best pillar pairing:** All pillars

### Social Footer
Instagram handle, website link, and unsubscribe. Drives social follows.

- **When to use:** Community/branded emails, UGC emails, any email where social engagement is a secondary goal
- **Best pillar pairing:** Community / Branded, Social Proof

### Branded Tagline Footer
"Powered by [Values], Mastered by [NAME]" with copyright. Adds a brand-voice touch to the sign-off.

- **When to use:** Brand story emails, founder messages, mission-driven emails
- **Best pillar pairing:** Community / Branded

---

## Building an Email: Module Stacking Examples

**Educational email (Myth-Busting):**
Standard Editorial Header + Myth-Busting Numbered List + Tip Callout Block + Trust Badge Strip + Standard Footer

**Sales email (Flash Sale):**
Countdown Timer Hero + Flash Sale Strip + Doorbuster Deals Grid + Free Shipping Banner + Social Footer

**Social Proof email:**
Standard Editorial Header + Customer Story Spotlight + Multi-Review Grid + Star Rating Bar + Single Product Spotlight + Standard Footer

**Product Launch email:**
New Drop Hero + Single Product Spotlight + Product Feature Breakdown + Repeat Buyer Badge + Trust Badge Strip + Standard Footer

**Gift Guide email:**
Gift Guide Hero + 4-Product Grid + Free Shipping Banner + Shipping Guide/Deadline Block + Standard Footer
